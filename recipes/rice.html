Fried Rice of Champions

A Delicious Wok of Fried Rice and a Classic Wooden Spoon

For me, fried rice has always been a comfort meal. It would be the only thing I could ever order at Asian restaurants and that may have annoyed a few people (cough cough mom). I don't know what it was, maybe the texture, maybe the act of ordering the same thing and always getting something different, I couldn't tell you. All I know is that fried rice was always something that I've loved.

Although, this may have changed in the past couple of years. My boyfriend makes the most delicious fried rice. I couldn't even begin to describe how much I love the way he makes it. From something that was just a comfort meal that made me feel safe to a meal that makes me feel excited. It's interesting, isn't it? How a great person can make anything great, just by touching it. They may never know but it will always be true. That might be the scaries part.

Ingredients

  • cooked rice
  • vegetables
  • oil
  • sauces and spices
  • eggs
  • meat of your choice
  • love

Steps

  1. start by heating up a wok with oil
  2. throw in the rice and let it cook a little before pushing it to the side
  3. after rice has been pushed, cook eggs in the center
  4. mix all the vegetables, meat and sauces together
  5. let cool and enjoy
