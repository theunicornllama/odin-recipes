Green Chili

Delicious Green chili topped with cilantro

This is authentically crafted green chili. My mom used to make green chili just like this when I was little, seeing pictures of it reminds me of the great memories my siblings and I made over dinner. Over the yelling and the bickering, the fighting and the teasing, all done over a delcious bowl of chili. Memories can often be forged in the dining room and I'm thankful that some of mine were.

For me, though, memories of the past are always so muddled in experiences of the present. I go to restaurants and order green chili only to be met with something that reminds me so little of my past that none of it is pleasant. It's hard to have memories and it's hard to live with them, especially when they're entangled with food as delcious as chili.

Ingredients

  • diced pork
  • flour
  • water
  • green chilis
  • spices
  • love

Steps

  1. take diced pork and throw into a hot pot
  2. once the pork is mostly cooked, we will throw flour in
  3. mix flour and pork grease until the flour isn't clumpy
  4. throw in chopped chilis, spices and all the love
  5. add water until desired consistency
  6. let cool and enjoy
